## Design Context

### Users
Readers (including the author) engaging with deep technical content (CS, Math, LLM). The reading context requires focus and clarity, but the experience should feel exceptionally comfortable, fresh, and elegant, rather than strictly clinical, heavy, or dry.

### Brand Personality
**雅致 (Elegant)**, **清新 (Fresh)**, **大方 (Generous/Atmospheric)**.
The tone is refined and uncluttered without being boring. It should never be flashy or overly decorated, but it must contain thoughtful, eye-catching microscopic details ("亮眼之处") that elevate the perceived quality.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Visual Tone**: Elevated minimalism (简约大气). Moving beyond "default minimal" into a thoughtfully spaced, structurally sound design.
- **Theme**: **Light Theme** focused.
- **Typography**: Elegant but highly legible. Moving away from standard utility sans-serifs into something more sophisticated—perhaps a serene serif for headings or a beautifully proportioned humanist sans, paired carefully to maintain readability for complex technical text.
- **Color**: A light, airy, and sophisticated palette. Colors will be custom-designed using OKLCH to feel fresh and bespoke. Neutrals will be subtly tinted rather than pure gray.

### Design Principles
1. **Elegant Restraint (雅致克制)**: Keep it clean and unflashy, but avoid "sterile" minimalism by introducing subtle richness in typography and spatial rhythm.
2. **Moments of Insight (亮眼细节)**: Introduce "eye-catching" details through refined micro-interactions, subtle color tints, or precise typographic flourishes, ensuring they support rather than distract from the content.
3. **Fresh & Airy Atmosphere (清新的呼吸感)**: Use generous spacing, fluid layouts, and perceptually balanced light colors to make reading complex Math and CS topics feel effortless.
4. **Sophisticated Legibility (高雅且清晰的排版)**: Select fonts that carry an elegant visual signature without compromising the clarity required for long-form technical writing and code blocks.
